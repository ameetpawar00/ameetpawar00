1.Open command promt(admin).
2.C:\Windows\system32>netsh wlan set hostednetwork mode=allow ssid=testwifi key=12345678
3.C:\Windows\system32>netsh wlan start hostednetwork
4.C:\Windows\system32>netsh wlan show hostednetwork
5.Right click on the network icon in the task bar and select network and sharing centre.
6.Click change adapter settings.
7.right click on ethernet and select properties then click sharing.
8.Tick on allow other users to connect through the computer's internet connection.
9.Then select local area connection just below from the drop down list.
10.OK

Enjoy your wifi hotspot..:))
